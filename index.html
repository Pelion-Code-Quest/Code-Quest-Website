Pelion Presents

Code Quest 2024

What

Code Quest is a competition for programmers in Utah.

The first round is remote on May 18.

The second round for finalists is on May 25 at Pelion HQ.

Registration

 Sign up here. 

Format

Round 1: May 18, 9:00am-12:30pm
Contestants submit solutions to 8 problems, similar to CodeChef, HackerRank, or other contests. Contestants may use their chosen language (Java, Python, Node.js, C#, Ruby, Go, C++, etc).
Scoring is the number of problems solved successfully. Ties are broken according to the time of the most recently solved problem.

Round 2: May 25, morning
The top twenty-five contestants from the first round will advance to the second round, on-site in Draper. Finalists will be provided with additional details.

Prizes

1st prize: $5000

2nd prize: $2500

3nd prize: $1000
